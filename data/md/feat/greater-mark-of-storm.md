# Greater Mark of Storm

Source: Eberron - Forge of the Artificer

_General Feat (Prerequisite: Level 4+, [Mark of Storm](/feat:mark-of-storm) Feat)_

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Improved Intuition.** When you use the Windwright’s Intuition benefit of your Mark of Storm feat, you can roll 1d6 instead of 1d4.

**Improved Storm.** When you use the Storm Magic benefit of your Mark of Storm feat to cast [Gust of Wind](/spell:gust-of-wind) without a spell slot, you also gain a Fly Speed of 60 feet for the duration of the spell.
