# Greater Mark of Shadow

Source: Eberron - Forge of the Artificer

_General Feat (Prerequisite: Level 4+, [Mark of Shadow](/feat:mark-of-shadow) Feat)_

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Improved Intuition.** When you use the Cunning Intuition benefit of your Mark of Shadow feat, you can roll 1d6 instead of 1d4.

**Improved Shadow.** When you use the Shape Shadows benefit of your Mark of Shadow feat to cast [Invisibility](/spell:invisibility) without a spell slot, you cast the spell as its level 3 version.
