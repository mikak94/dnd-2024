# Watchers

Source: Ravenloft - The Horrors Within

_Dark Gift Feat (Prerequisite: Ravenloft Campaign)_

Something unnatural is always watching you, taking the form of scurrying vermin and other eerie creatures. You gain the following features.

**Borrowed Eyes.** You always have the [Beast Sense](/spell:beast-sense) and [Speak with Animals](/spell:speak-with-animals) spells prepared. You can cast each spell without a spell slot, and you must finish a Long Rest before you can cast it in this way again. You can also cast these spells using spell slots you have of the appropriate level.

**Heightened Suspicion.** Whenever you take the Search action, you can roll 1d4 and add the number rolled to any ability check made as part of that action.

**Incessant Watchers.** You have Disadvantage on saving throws made against the [Scrying](/spell:scrying) spell.

In addition, immediately after you make a D20 Test and roll a 1 on the d20, paranoia threatens to overwhelm you. Make a Wisdom saving throw (DC 13 plus your Proficiency Bonus). On a failed save, you have Disadvantage on D20 Tests for 1 minute. You can repeat the save at the end of each of your turns, ending the effect early on a success.
