# Greater Mark of Healing

Source: Eberron - Forge of the Artificer

_General Feat (Prerequisite: Level 4+, [Mark of Healing](/feat:mark-of-healing) Feat)_

You gain the following benefits.

**Ability Score Increase.** Increase one ability score of your choice by 1, to a maximum of 20.

**Improved Intuition.** When you use the Medical Intuition benefit of your Mark of Healing feat, you can roll 1d6 instead of 1d4.

**Improved Healing.** You can now use the Healing Touch benefit of your Mark of Healing feat to cast [Cure Wounds](/spell:cure-wounds) without using a spell slot a number of times equal to your Proficiency Bonus, and you regain all expended uses when you finish a Long Rest.

Additionally, when you cast [Cure Wounds](/spell:cure-wounds) and roll dice to determine the number of Hit Points restored, you can treat any 1 or 2 on a roll as a 3.
