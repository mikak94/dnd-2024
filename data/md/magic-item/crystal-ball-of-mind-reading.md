# Crystal Ball of Mind Reading

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

_Wondrous Item, Legendary (Requires Attunement)_

While touching this crystal orb, you can cast [Scrying](/spell:scrying) (save DC 17) with it. In addition, you can cast [Detect Thoughts](/spell:detect-thoughts) (save DC 17) targeting creatures you can see within 30 feet of the spell’s sensor. You don’t need to concentrate on this _Detect Thoughts_ spell to maintain it during its duration, but it ends if the _Scrying_ spell ends.
