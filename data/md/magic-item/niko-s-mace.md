# Niko's Mace

Source: Uni and the Hunt for the Lost Horn

_Weapon (Mace), Very Rare (Requires Attunement by a Spellcaster)_

This Mace has 6 charges and regains 1d6 expended charges daily at dawn. While holding the Mace, you can expend 1 of its charges to cast [Summon Celestial](/spell:summon-celestial) (+9 to hit with spell attacks).
