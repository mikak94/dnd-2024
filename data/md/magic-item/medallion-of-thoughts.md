# Medallion of Thoughts

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

_Wondrous Item, Uncommon (Requires Attunement)_

The medallion has 5 charges. While wearing it, you can expend 1 charge to cast [Detect Thoughts](/spell:detect-thoughts) (save DC 13) from it. The medallion regains 1d4 expended charges daily at dawn.
