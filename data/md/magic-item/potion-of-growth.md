# Potion of Growth

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

_Potion, Uncommon_

When you drink this potion, you gain the “enlarge” effect of the [Enlarge/Reduce](/spell:enlarge-reduce) spell for 10 minutes (no Concentration required).

The red in the potion’s liquid continuously expands from a tiny bead to color the clear liquid around it and then contracts. Shaking the bottle fails to interrupt this process.
