# Potion of Animal Friendship

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

_Potion, Uncommon_

When you drink this potion, you can cast the level 3 version of the [Animal Friendship](/spell:animal-friendship) spell (save DC 13).

Agitating this potion’s muddy liquid brings little bits into view: a fish scale, a hummingbird feather, a cat claw, or a squirrel hair.
