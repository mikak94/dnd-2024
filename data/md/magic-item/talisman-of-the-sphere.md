# Talisman of the Sphere

Source: Dungeon Master's Guide

_Wondrous Item, Legendary (Requires Attunement)_

While holding or wearing this talisman, you have Advantage on any Intelligence (Arcana) check you make to control a [Sphere of Annihilation](/magic-item:sphere-of-annihilation). In addition, when you start your turn in control of a _Sphere of Annihilation_, you can take a Magic action to move it 10 feet plus a number of additional feet equal to 10 times your Intelligence modifier. This movement doesn’t have to be in a straight line.
