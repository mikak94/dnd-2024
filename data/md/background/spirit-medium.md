# Spirit Medium

Source: Ravenloft - The Horrors Within

Through strange rituals and fateful encounters, you’ve discovered that you bear a unique connection to the spirits of the dead and damned. Your body is a conduit for such spirits, granting you prescient insight from beyond. But these spirits’ intentions aren’t always innocent, and their gifts come at a haunting cost.

**Ability Scores:** Constitution, Intelligence, Wisdom  
**Feat:** [Dark Gift](/feat:all#toc6) feat of your choice (see “Feats”; [Gathered Whispers](/feat:gathered-whispers) is recommended)  
**Skill Proficiencies:** Insight and Religion  
**Tool Proficiencies:** Choose one kind of Gaming Set  
**Equipment:** Choose A or B: (A) Dagger, Gaming Set (same as above), Basket, Bell, 8 Candles, Ink, Ink Pen, Paper (5 sheets), Tinderbox, Traveler’s Clothes, and 32 GP; or (B) 50 GP
