# Haunted One

Source: Ravenloft - The Horrors Within

You are haunted by the events of your past. Whether it was a lone terrible incident or an accumulation of painful moments, you bear the unshakable weight of what happened to you. You’ve tried to bury it and run away from it, to no avail; it can’t be slain with a sword or banished via magic. Nevertheless, you persist.

**Ability Scores:** Constitution, Wisdom, Charisma  
**Feat:** [Survivor](/feat:survivor) or a [Dark Gift](/feat:all#toc6) feat of your choice  
**Skill Proficiencies:** Arcana and Survival  
**Tool Proficiencies:** Choose one kind of Gaming Set  
**Equipment:** Choose A or B: (A) Gaming Set (same as above), Crowbar, Holy Water (1 flask), Mirror, Oil(2 flasks), Signal Whistle, Tinderbox, Traveler’s Clothes, 5 Torches, Waterskin, and 14 GP; or (B) 50 GP
